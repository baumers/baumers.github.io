About

Hello! I'm a traditional neuroscientist by training and love using research as a tool to better understand the world around us. My research projects connect across linguistics, computer science, and electrical engineering to answer research questions. In particular I'm interested in individual differences in the way we understand and process information. Learn more about my work by checking out my past publications. I'm off to my next adventure and leaving academia! I can't wait to dive into User Experience (UX) research and apply my background in perception to new problems. You can find my resume here.
